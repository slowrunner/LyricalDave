The GoPiGo3 robot has a safe navigation radius of 15 cm.  If the distance sensor reading is greater than 30 cm, announce "Im going to explore now" and drive forward at 0.15 meters per second until the distance sensor reading is less than or equal 20 cm, then announce "obstacle ahead", and wait 5 seconds.  After the wait, turn 180 degrees and announce "nobody better bother me here."
